Project Specs

Defeating Eroom's Law

Eroom's Law states drug discovery becomes slower and more expensive over time, despite technology. We aim to reverse the process.

The Problem with the 'Black Box'

90% of drugs fail in clinical trials. Traditional AI platforms provide correlation without causation. RepurposeAI was built to solve the "Black Box" transparency problem. By employing a multi-agent framework across biological, regulatory, patent, and clinical domains simultaneously, we calculate human-readable Reasoning Paths for every drug candidate, validating the why not just the what.

Intellectual Freedom

We synthesize patent data alongside biochemical data to expose high-potential compounds that are Free-To-Operate in the public domain.

Academic Enablement

Designed for leading research institutes, our goal is to empower a decentralized network of independent researchers to circumvent legacy big-pharma bottlenecks.

Guidance & Support

How to get the most out of the drug discovery platform.

  • 1. Starting a Deep Dive

    Go to the Repurpose tab, enter a molecule name, and wait for the 6 agents to finish their retrieval. The AI will then synthesize a full hypothesis.

  • 2. Understanding Confidence

    The Confidence Score (0-100%) reflects the richness of data found across all domains. Higher scores indicate stronger clinical and patent evidence.

  • 3. Knowledge Test

    Use the Test tab to challenge your clinical intuition against our AI results. This is recommended for students to improve their pharmacology logic.
